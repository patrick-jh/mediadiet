About My Media Diet

My Media Diet is a personal project designed to help me keep track and analyze what I watch, read, and listen to. Mostly, I wanted a way to remember if I had already seen "that movie" or listened to "that album". Plus, I also wanted to see if I was using one streaming service more than another.

My method

  • Media Entry Logging: Easily log details about movies, TV shows, books, music (and more) consumed.
  • Comprehensive Statistics: View detailed statistics about media consumption patterns, keep track of ratings, genres, foreign media, did I like it or did I not finish it?
  • Regarding entries: For TV shows, I logged whenever I watched episodes for a specific series. So I may have watched more than one episode for an entry. And for recommended, mostly, these more things that I liked, and would recommend at the very least, to some people.
